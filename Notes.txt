https://chris-vermeulen.com/laravel-in-kubernetes/

Install laravel:
curl -s "https://laravel.build/laravelkube" | bash
cd laravelkube
APP_PORT=8003 ./vendor/bin/sail up
http://localhost:8003


docker exec -it laravelkube_mysql_1 sh

Stop sail:
./vendor/bin/sail stop

Remove container:
./vendor/bin/sail down
